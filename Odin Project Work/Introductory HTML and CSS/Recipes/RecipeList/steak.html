Steak

A high quality cut of meat, cooked to perfection!

List of ingredients

  • A nice piece of steak!

Preparation method

  1. Season the meat with salt and pepper.
  2. Place onto a pan a heated pan and allow the meat to sear.
  3. After searing one side for 3 minutes, flip to sear the other side.
  4. Enjoy!
